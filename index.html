I am a 3rd-year doctoral student at Brown University, advised by Shriram Krishnamurthi. Here I did some works on type system × tabular data and some works on gradual typing × Python. Now I am working on CSEd Research. More specifically, I focus on teaching the semantics of programming languages.

I was a MS CS student at Indiana University. I worked on Gradual Typing and relational programming. My master's thesis advisor is Jeremy Siek.
